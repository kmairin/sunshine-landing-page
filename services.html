Tips for Your Visit

  • Sort before you come: Separate whites, darks, and delicates at home to save time at the laundromat.
  • Bring your detergent: While you can use any detergent, bringing your own preferred brand ensures the best results.
  • Don't overload machines: Leave room for clothes to tumble freely for the best clean.
  • Use the right machine size: Save money by matching your load to the right washer — don't pay for capacity you don't need.
  • Set a timer: Stay aware of when your load finishes so you can move it promptly. Other customers will appreciate it!
  • Check pockets: Always empty pockets before washing to avoid losing items or damaging machines.
