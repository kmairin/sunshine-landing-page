Our Story

Built for the Community

Sunshine Coin Laundry LLC was founded in 2021 with a simple mission: to give the South Los Angeles community a laundromat they can be proud of. We saw too many outdated, poorly maintained facilities in the neighborhood and knew our residents deserved better.

Located at 10808 S Vermont Ave, our facility was designed from the ground up to be clean, bright, and welcoming. We invested in modern, energy-efficient machines that get your clothes cleaner and dryer faster — saving you both time and money.

From families washing a week's worth of laundry to travelers passing through Los Angeles, we serve everyone with the same care and attention. Our on-site attendants keep the space spotless and are always happy to help.

Our Community

Part of the South LA Family

Sunshine Coin Laundry is proud to be part of the vibrant South Los Angeles community. Our customers come from the surrounding neighborhoods — Vermont-Slauson, Westmont, Athens, Willowbrook, Broadway-Manchester, and beyond.

We believe a clean laundromat contributes to a stronger, healthier community. That's why we go beyond just providing machines — we create a comfortable space where neighbors can meet, catch up, and take care of an essential part of daily life.

Have questions or want to learn more? Get in touch with us — we'd love to hear from you.
