About Us

Your Neighborhood Laundromat in South Los Angeles

Since 2021, Sunshine Coin Laundry has been serving the Los Angeles community with a clean, modern, and welcoming laundry experience. Located on Vermont Ave in the heart of South LA, we provide top-quality machines, a comfortable atmosphere, and the best value in the neighborhood.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our laundromat.

What are your hours of operation?

Sunshine Coin Laundry is open 7 days a week, Monday through Sunday, from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We're here whenever you need to get your laundry done — early morning or late evening.

Do you have large-capacity washing machines?

Yes! We have a variety of machine sizes including large-capacity washers perfect for comforters, blankets, sleeping bags, and bulk laundry loads. Check out our services page for more details on machine sizes.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer free on-site parking right outside our facility for all customers. There's plenty of space, and it's easy to pull up and unload your laundry.

Do you have WiFi?

Absolutely! We provide free WiFi for all customers so you can browse, stream, work, or catch up on emails while you wait for your laundry.

Do I need to bring my own quarters?

No need to come prepared with a bag of quarters. We have coin changer machines on site that convert your bills into quarters quickly. Just bring cash and you're all set.

Is there an attendant on site?

Yes, we have a friendly attendant on site during all operating hours to help with machine questions, provide assistance, and keep the facility clean and safe. Feel free to ask them anything!

Where exactly is Sunshine Coin Laundry located?

We are located at 10808 S Vermont Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90044, in the Vermont-Slauson neighborhood of South Los Angeles. We're easy to find — right on Vermont Ave with free parking out front. Get detailed directions here.
